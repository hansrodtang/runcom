Runcom

A Dotfiles manager written in Go.

Still in heavy development and is not ready for usage. Please wait for the 1.0 release

Installation

Prebuilt executables are available here.

  go get github.com/hansrodtang/runcom

For those who prefer it, you can also use gopkg.in:

  go get gopkg.in/hansrodtang/runcom.v0
